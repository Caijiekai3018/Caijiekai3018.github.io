Elements in HTML: Audio

Hypertext Markup Language Label

Path: html > p01 > 01

<audio> is a label to load the sounds in browser.

The common form of <audio> like mp3, ogg and etc..

Notes

To use this element, you must run it UNDER HTML.

Here is three tests of sounds.

Click the link under this word to start.



Grammar:
<audio src=" " loop=" " controls=" " preload=" " muted=" ">
loop: Loop the audio if the audio was finished.
controls: Show the controller of audio.
preload: Controls the audio load.
muted: Controls the audio's volume.
src: This label controls the audio's source from. (MUST)

Example: These sound use these code:
<audio src="sounds\Komorebi.mp3" preload="auto" controls="control">
<audio src="sounds\Never gonna give you up.mp3" preload="auto" controls="control">
<audio src="sounds\The Spectre.flac" preload="auto" controls="control">
