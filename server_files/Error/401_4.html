401.4 Unsupport Device

If you see this problem first time, just close this page then refresh it. This could not take a long time to implement, but it is important.

If you see this error page again, you maybe check your connect. Just follow these step:
  1. Check your connect. This step is to determine if your device has a stable connection to the server.
  2. Check your telecom subscription. This step is to check if you have access to the network.
  3. Check the firewall. This step is to determine if your connection is blocked by your computer's firewall or the device imformation was sent by wrong way.
  4. Replace the device. Maybe the error is due to the device, just change to a better device and re-enter the webpage.

If you call a support person, please provide the following error codes:
     Error code: UNSUPPORT_DEVICE_LOGIN_INTO_SERVER (401.4)
